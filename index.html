ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Ramzy El-Taher and I am from Fort Myers, FL. I am 24 years old and currently a Software Engineering student at FGCU. Throughout my time at FGCU, I have gained a lot of experience creating software, mobile apps, websites, and games for projects that I can display to others. I have experience programming in many different languages such as Java, C++, C#, and I have used a lot of different IDEs to build software in such as IntelliJ, Unity, and Android Studio. Some of my favorite projects involved working with JavaFX and IntelliJ to create a Production Line Tracker, as well as using Unity and C# to develop games.

I am currently a Learning Assistant in the Software Engineering program at FGCU, and I have helped the Chair of Software Engineering with his Introduction to Programming class for one semester so far. I am also the Secretary of the Software Engineering Club at the university, and I have helped the club organized their weekly meetings and the monthly events that they usually host throughout each semester. Their meetings usually consist of either social settings or a topic that members request to know more about such as Git, and their monthly events usually consists of speakers from different software companies who come to talk to the club about their company and what they do.
